4 Key Discoveries

Discovery #1
The STP Reality Gap (Motor)
EU convention systems could auto-resolve 60-70% of motor claims. Only ~10% actually achieve STP. The convention lookup is trivial — a database query. The bottleneck is data quality: ~50% of EAS forms are still paper, filled out by stressed people with shaking hands. Whoever fixes data quality upstream unlocks the 50+ point gap.
Discovery #2
EU vs US Are Fundamentally Different
EU: single agreed document (EAS) signed by both parties → convention fault lookup. US: competing narratives, no joint document → human investigation every time. EU FNOL = verification. US FNOL = data collection. Different automation opportunities in each.
Discovery #3
Home Claims Are Structurally Different
Motor has a counterparty and an agreed document (EAS). Home usually has neither. Motor question: "who caused it?" Home question: "was it covered?" Unlike motor, EU has no structural advantage for home claims. The opportunity is "make adjusters 3x more productive," not "unlock STP."
Discovery #4
AI Replacing Adjusters Is Overhyped (Home)
AI can detect visible damage and process documents. It cannot see hidden damage (water behind walls, mold), determine causation ("sudden burst" vs "gradual leak"), or handle complex coverage interpretation. Current accuracy ~70%, needs >95%. Also: AI is making fraud EASIER. Realistic target: augment adjusters (3x productivity), not replace them.

Key Decisions Made

✓US-centric first
Call-based workflow fits US market. EU parallel with GDPR compliance (copilot fallback if consent denied).
✓Motor + Property
Same architecture, different SOPs. Doubles SOP work but widens addressable market.
✓Per-claim pricing
EUR 15-30 / USD equivalent per claim processed.
✓CMS integration in MVP
Without it, adjuster re-enters data manually — kills adoption. Moved from "later" to "must have."
✓Revised MVP scope
Blocks 1+2+5+6: Call Assistant + SOP Engine + Decision Capture + basic CMS push. 4-5 months.
✓Block 3 (Doc Intelligence)
Evaluate build vs partner with Sprout.ai/Roots Automation before committing.

Open Questions for Co-Founder

1. Which CMS does the existing insurance customer use?
Determines Block 6 (CMS integration) target. Guidewire? Duck Creek? Custom?
2. Build vs partner for document AI (Block 3)?
Sprout.ai and Roots Automation are options. Building adds 3-4 months; partnering means dependency.
3. Motor-only MVP, or motor + property from day one?
Adding property doubles SOP authoring work but broadens the market immediately.
4. Which transcription provider?
Deepgram vs AssemblyAI vs Whisper. Affects latency, accuracy, and cost of the call assistant.

Product Vision

One-liner
"Adjuster operating system that captures decision ground truth"
Not replacing adjusters — making them 3x more productive while capturing every decision as structured data. This data becomes the moat: train models on real adjuster decisions, not synthetic data.

| **Large** | €200M-€1B | ~30-47 | Zurich PT, Linea Directa, Matmut, Vittoria, HDI | Buy best-of-breed or TPA outsource | YES — longer cycles |
| **Caravela tier** | €100-€200M | **~96-165** | Caravela, Pelayo, Thelem, many French mutuelles | **Need it, can't build it. ~100-200 employees.** | **PRIMARY TARGET** |
| **Small** | €50-€100M | **~204-260+** | UNA (PT), Patria Hispana (ES), small mutuelles (FR) | Want tech, tight budget, <100 employees | YES — entry pricing |
| **Micro** | <€50M | ~243-372+ | Regional mutuals, niche writers, captives | Manual processes, minimal tech, <50 staff | LONG-TAIL only |

**Why "Caravela tier" (€100-200M) is the sweet spot:**
- Big enough to have real claims volume (~30-60K/year) that justifies the investment
- Small enough to lack a data science team or in-house AI capability
- Growing fast (Caravela grew 10x in 10 years) — need tools to scale without proportional headcount
- Budget exists: €50K-200K/year is 0.03-0.2% of GWP — trivial if it improves STP or reserve accuracy
- Decision-making is fast: 1-2 decision-makers, not a 12-month procurement process

### What Nobody Is Building

1. **Coverage determination AI** — reads a policy, matches against claim facts, determines "is this covered?"
2. **Causation analysis** — "sudden vs gradual" for water damage (#1 disputed area in property)
3. **Convention system matching** — EAS data extraction + IRSA/CARD/CIDE/IDS fault lookup (EU motor)
4. **SOP-driven workflows** — carrier-specific decision processes, not generic playbooks
5. **Reserve recommendation with P&L framing** — CFO-level selling point, not just adjuster tool
6. **Decision ground truth capture** — WHY adjusters decide, not just outcomes

## 8. Key Takeaways

1. **Total market**: ~80-95M claims/year, €700B+ in premiums globally
2. **Target segment**: ~11-28M claims/year, ~360-505 carriers + 500-800 TPAs
3. **Target as % of total**: ~14-29% — large enough for $100M+ ARR, small enough that enterprise competitors aren't competing for the same buyers
4. **Same buyers for motor + home**: 80-95% carrier overlap. Property is an upsell (+60-70% revenue), not new customer acquisition
5. **Market is wide open**: 90-95% EU mid-market has no claims AI. Property decision layer is ~99% empty
6. **The wedge**: EU motor convention systems (start with Portugal/Caravela, expand to Spain/France/Italy)
7. **The company**: Motor + property decision engine across EU and US mid-market. €160-425M target TAM, €320M-1.1B full addressable
